Support

The Noise-Planet team can provide professional support for both NoiseCapture and NoiseModelling.
Below are listed the different elements we can propose for both tools.

NoiseCapture

  • Turnkey party: just tell us when and where the party will be. We organize and conduct the party with attendees, we provide the online map and we bring our reference sonometers.
  • Help for party: want to create your own party but just need some help. We can assist you in different ways.
  • Dedicated map: an online and interactive map, feeded by NoiseCapture measures, feeting with your needs (e.g on a specific territory, with a specific logo or text, ...).
  • Your all-in-one dataset: a dedicated extraction of the NoiseCapture database. Just tell us when and where have been made the needed measures. We can also provide additionnal informations to helps you better exploit these data.

NoiseModelling

  • Training, from the theory of sound propagation to the practical application with the tool and concrete examples.
  • Development of new functionality or consideration of new noise emission sources.

Interested?

If want to know more, feel free to contact us. We will be happy to offer you tailor-made solutions, adapted to your needs.
