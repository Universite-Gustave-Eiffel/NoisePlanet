NoiseModelling day 2021

If you see this message, you are invited to participate to the “NoiseModelling day 2021” on June 9th, 2021 from 10am to 4pm (French, UTC+2) in the form of a webinar.
In 2020, we organized the first NoiseModelling days in the form of a general presentation of the tool and tutorials.
This year we will focus on the user:

  • The morning will be dedicated to the presentation of the 2021 novelties and to a series of user presentations in "flash" format (5 min + 3 min questions) or "classic" format (15 min + 5 min questions).
  • In the afternoon, we will continue user presentations and discuss future development needs.

The final goal off this event is to federate a community around NoiseModelling and to better meet the needs of users.

Participate

Participation in the seminar is free of charge but registration is required at the following link: https://forms.gle/svkB9NixcN6Kpodc6.
Presenters must register by May 1, 2021 and other participants by May 23, 2021.

Looking forward to meet you (even virtually)!
